Front Controls
Overview

The Front Controls show a list of the high and low pressures and fronts in a textual list format.

Double clicking on an element will show its properties editor through which you can view its times and change visibility, position, or pressure values.

Properties
  • Z Position

    • Allows you to define what is the fixed Z position.
