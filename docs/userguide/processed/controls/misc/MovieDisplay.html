QuickTime Movie Controls
Overview

McIDAS-V can display QuickTime movies. You can pause, single step and play a movie.

Properties
  • Play - Click to start the QuickTime movie.
  • Pause - Click to pause the QuickTime movie.
  • Step Backward - Click to move backwards a single step in the movie.
  • Step Forward - Click to move forwards a single step in the movie.
  • Media Properties - Left-click to bring up the QuickTime media properties. Right-click to change the rate of the movie.

    The media properties is composed of three tabs. The first display the general information about the QuickTime movie, including location, duration, and frame rate.

    The second lists the specific video information, including encoding type and size.

    The third has access to the PlugIn Viewer details.
